My Favorite Halloween Costume

I would like to write about my favorite Halloween costume I have ever made. In my sophomore year of high school, I had the genius idea to dress up as a Gucci flip flop. I gathered all of the supplies and spent 4 hours making my costume. I used black posterboard, red tape, green tape, and foam. I wrote "GUCCI" on the back in large gold letters to really seal the deal. It took way too much effort, but the costume was a big hit.

I remember going out the night that I made it. It was a large costume, so it was quite immobile. I had to put it in the trunk of the car and put it on when we got to the house party. When we were inside, I could barely fit through the door. It was all worth it though. Everyone around me was dressed as something normal: a pirate, alien, teacher, but me? I was a freaking Gucci flip flop. I personally believe that I single-handedly won Halloween that year, and I will never live it down.
